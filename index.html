The Web3 Space Industry

SpaceRanch DAO is the land management organization intended to purchase, develop and operate properties facilitating space launch, development and tourism.

We are embarking on an exciting new frontier where blockchain technologies collide with the emerging space industry. Our developments will innovate how individuals can collaborate, fund, and vote towards the proliferation of the space age within this new economy.

The SpaceRanch is registered as a DAO LLC in the state of Wyoming, and serves the purpose of defining the spacetech and fintech corridor between Cheyanne and Colorado Springs. It is our mission to expand the space exploration capabilities within the region by curating the facilites necessary for the space ecosystem.
